Omelette

Description

Who hasn't heard of the beautiful fully omelette? A friend for any occassion, as many eggs as you want, any ingredient you want and bam;meal

They say you can't make an omelette without breaking some eggs, and they would be right!

Ingredients

  • Eggs, 2 to 3 big ones
  • Butter
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Anything you want

Recipe

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl, beat together with salt and pepper
  2. Gently warm a small frying pan, melt the butter and spread it around the pan
  3. Add the beaten eggs, gently mix the eggs around the pan warming the mixture
  4. Continue to gently stir until the eggs start to set
  5. Add your choice of ingredients, cheese or ham!
  6. Gently fold over the eggs when set over the added ingredients
  7. cook until set
  8. Season to taste!
